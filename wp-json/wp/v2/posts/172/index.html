In my childhood, I didn’t know about kashful stories or association with Durga Puja, so it was ignored, only I noticed a kashful nearby idol when it\u2019s decked up and ready for Puja. During Autumn’s season when I used to go to school, the most preferred and best transport was cycling and walking. My School was situated beside the river. While I was travelling for school this Autumn Season and in other seasons also I saw White grass around the bank of the river and a corner of different portions. That time I didn’t feel about Kashful, maybe no one told me or I experienced such nostalgic and aesthetic pleasure of it.
