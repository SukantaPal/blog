Karwa Chauth (\u0915\u0930\u0935\u093e \u091a\u094c\u0925) is one of the major festivals, celebrated widely throughout the country, in such pandemic situations at home, abiding all covid safety norms, in new normal way. Its said that while gods had a battle with Ashuras, the Goddess Parvati, Lakshmi, Saraswati and others kept fast for their husbands and wished for their victory over evil power. Since then women on this day fast and pray for their husband’s long life and safety.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
