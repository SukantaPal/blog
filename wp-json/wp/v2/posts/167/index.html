After Jhulan Purnima or Jhulan Utsav, one of the main and biggest festivals of Vaishnava traditions (Vaishnavism is one of the major Hindu denominations along with Shaivism, Shaktism, and Smartism) is Krishna Janmashtami, also known simply as Janmashtami or Gokulashtami, is an annual Hindu festival that celebrates the birth of Krishna, the eighth avatar of Vishnu. This Year 30th August 2021 celebrated Krishna Janmashtami or the Birthday of Krishna. This festival is celebrated particularly in Mathura and Vrindavan, but lots of households in different countries celebrated in their way. In Kolkata, lots of heritage houses or bonedi bari and Mandir or Temple equally celebrated Janmashtami. Early Morning Gaudia Math Bagbazar started their procession, Afternoon Partha Sarthai Mandir Started Competition for kids of becoming a character of Krishna.
